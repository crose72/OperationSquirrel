About Operation Squirrel

Open-source robotics built for schools, students, and aspiring engineers.

The Mission

Operation Squirrel was created to give students access to the same tools, algorithms, and engineering workflows used in the robotics industry. Everything is open-source, modifiable, and designed to support real-world learning.

Why We Built This

Most educational robotics kits hide the real engineering behind closed systems. Operation Squirrel is different — every part of the stack is exposed, from AI detection to flight control to onboard Linux systems.

Students learn real autonomy, not toy-level coding.
